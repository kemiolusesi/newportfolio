Project Details
  • Case Study: Refining a Comprehensive Academic Guide for Global Readership
  • Client: Confidential
  • Date: 25 January, 2024

Client Overview:
A widely published author enlisted editorial support for a manuscript intended for broad, international circulation. With the work already rich in voice and insight, the goal was to refine its clarity, consistency, and strategic direction for its intended audience.

Editorial Approach:
The project focused on refining tone and messaging across chapters while preserving the originality of the work.
Through constructive criticism, I worked carefully on line editing and tonal recalibration, in efforts to strengthen the narrative flow, enhance structural cohesion, and ensure the language meets professional publishing standards.
Working closely with the author, I reviewed the manuscript section by section, identifying inconsistencies, clarifying transitions, and refining word choice to align with the intended audience and support diverse comprehension levels.
