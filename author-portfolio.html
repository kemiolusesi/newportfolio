Project Details
  • Case Study: Turning Words into Lasting Legacies
  • Client: Just a Few Friends
  • Date: 23 November, 2018
  • Website: www.justafewfriends.com

Client Overview:
A talented writer with a passion for storytelling wanted to expand her reach and broaden her audience. However, she had long struggled with visibility, hesitant to put herself out there. Over time, she lost some of her most cherished pieces due to technological mishaps, reinforcing her fears of navigating the digital space. Beyond a website, she needed a secure, lasting home for her works.

Challenges:
~ A lack of digital presence made her value inaccessible.
~ A Fear of visibility held her refrained from embracing her potential.
~ Past data loss created distrust in online platforms.

Solutions:
Branding & Design
We built an identity around her bold, eloquent, and timeless voice, employing design elements, from typography to color palettes, to ensure her work imprinted it's intrinsic essence.
Web Development & Maintenance
We developed a secure & high-performing WordPress-powered website with custom integration for enhanced functionality and optimally displayed for a global audience.
Author Services & Digital Presence
To bridge the gap between value and audience, we implemented SEO strategies, content structuring, and social media integrations to empower professional growth.

Key Takeaway
By addressing not just the technical needs but also the emotional barriers, we helped transform hesitation into confidence and vulnerability into visibility. This project proved that when creativity meets the right digital strategy, the impact is not just seen—it is felt.
