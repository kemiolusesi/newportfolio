Hello!

I am Kemi Olusesi — a full-stack developer, creative, and digital strategist working remotely from Kano, Nigeria, with a background in clinical nutrition.
I build purposeful digital products that bridge creativity, functionality, and global relevance.

What began as a curiosity for web development has evolved into a career focused on merging technology, design, and strategic thinking to solve real-world challenges.
For nine years, I have built a strength around content strategy; crafting brand messaging, managing projects, and developing web solutions that often lead to full brand revamps.
This foundation also enables me to support authors through end-to-end publishing services, from formatting and editing to cover design and KDP distribution.

I’ve likewise led as a Project Manager for community-driven programs, Africa institutional summits, leadership forums, and public health initiatives—sharpening my skills in budgeting, team coordination, and cross-functional project delivery. Along the way, I’ve earned certifications in Human Resources and Business Analysis, further strengthening my leadership and operational expertise.

At the heart of everything I do is a passion for impact. My long-term goal is to integrate AI and machine learning into practical solutions that address global health disparities, especially within nutrition-focused interventions, and to lead tech empowerment initiatives in Northern Nigeria. I’m currently expanding my knowledge in these areas while helping clients bring their bold ideas to life.

Outside of work, I enjoy supporting community-centered initiatives through food drives, public health outreach, and facilitating tech empowerment programs. I also have a soft spot for good music and a habit of experimenting (and sometimes making a mess!) with new recipes in the kitchen.

I’m always open to meaningful collaborations that creates lasting value.

Let’s build something impactful together.
