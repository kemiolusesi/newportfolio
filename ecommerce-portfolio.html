Project Details
  • Case Study: Building a Digital Home for Published Works
  • Client: Prints by Liore
  • Date: 23 November, 2022
  • Website: www.printsbyliore.com

Client Overview:
A multi-title author was ready to take her publishing journey beyond Amazon by creating a central, branded space to host her growing portfolio of books. She needed a professional web presence that connected her storytelling voice to a global audience—and converted interest into book sales.

Challenges:
~ No standalone author website to showcase her works.
~ Limited discoverability and brand cohesion across platforms.
~ No integration between her content and her Amazon listings

Solutions:
Book Design & Visual Identity
We refined her branding by designing consistent, genre-appropriate book covers and complementary digital assets that extended across her site and marketing content. The interior layout for each book was designed to ensure readability,functionality and a polished user experience.
Publishing Execution & Market Readiness
We formatted her manuscripts to meet Amazon KDP requirements, managed metadata, optimized descriptions, and assisted with uploading and publishing, ensuring each book was retail-ready for both print and eBook distribution.
eCommerce Integration & WordPress Development
A custom WordPress website was developed using a combination of eCommerce plugins and marketplace integrations. Each book was presented on its own page with summaries, reviews, and direct links to Amazon purchase options. We built an intuitive catalog system, ensuring readers could browse titles by genre or theme.

Key Takeaway
By combining design, publishing, and web development expertise, we created a platform where her product thrives, grows, and sells. This project taught me the power of blending creative vision with strategic functionality to empower authors beyond the bookstore.
